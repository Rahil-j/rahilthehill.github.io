About us!

SSIS golf is a programme at the heart of SSIS, located in the Minhang district, it is a perfect location to come with a few friends and put some balls in holes! EIGHT OF THEM! And thats not only it, there is a massive driving range where you can hit the balls as hard as possible, whether it be practicing your drive or your putting skills, its the best place to hangout and play. And we almost forgot, we are not just a Golf location, but a international school and a residence area! You can use the Official website link above in your menu bar to visit it!
